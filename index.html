What is the KMMC 2?

The KMMC 2 (Karate Masters Mathematics Competitions 2) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the Art of Problem Solving user karate7800 that makes original contests at a level suitable for elementary schoolers. As well, all contests feature a Challenge Mode, which offers a unique speed-based format for older audiences.
